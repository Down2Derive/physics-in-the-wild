Current Research Directions

Instrumentation, Cosmic Signals, and Computational Astrophysics

nEXO Charge Amplification Design

Advancing detector hardware for the next-generation neutrinoless double beta decay search. I collaborate with SLAC engineers to prototype a charge amplification component that can be scaled to the full nEXO detector, using Solidworks, COMSOL, and precision bench testing.

Project details

Dark Matter Image Analysis

Built Python-based pipelines to process astronomical imagery, suppress noise, and surface subtle dark matter signatures during my SLAC research traineeship. The workflow accelerated our ability to inspect candidate events in large observational datasets.

Summer research notes

LSST Star–Galaxy Classification

Developing fast machine learning classifiers through the LSSTC Data Science Fellowship. By integrating NumPy- and scikit-learn–driven models, I’m improving early-stage separation of stellar and galactic sources for Rubin Observatory surveys.
